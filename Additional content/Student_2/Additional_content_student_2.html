Methane flaring under the ice 🔥💨

The ice contains vast stores of organic carbon and methane, created by the archaea of ​ the flourishing flora and marine life that flourished during the Paleocene-Eocene thermal maximum 55 million years ago.

As the climate gradually cooled, the remains of soil, plants, and marine life became sediments trapped far below the ice. Part of the clean microbes converted into methane, a powerful greenhouse gas.😯💥

Submarine Armor

When Harry L. Bowdoin applied for a patent for his submarine armor in 1914, the diving world was not well advanced.

On this occasion he abandoned his project as it did not meet with the success he had hoped for. Perhaps because he was technologically ahead of his time and the technical and physical factors such as light materials, lighting, breathing apparatus and everything else necessary for a successful dive were not up to date.
